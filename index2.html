Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Formation: Teams can consist of 2 to 4 members. You can come with a pre-formed team or find members at the event.
  • Project Originality: All projects must be built from scratch during the 24-hour hackathon period. Use of pre-existing code is prohibited, though open-source libraries and frameworks are allowed.
  • Code of Conduct: All participants must adhere to a strict code of conduct. Be respectful, inclusive, and collaborative. Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated and will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Submission: Each team must submit their project, including a link to a public code repository (e.g., GitHub) and a short presentation/demo video, before the final deadline.
  • Judging Criteria: Projects will be judged based on technical complexity, innovation, design, real-world applicability, and the quality of the final presentation.
  • Intellectual Property: You own what you build. Your team retains full intellectual property rights for your project.
  • Final Decision: The decisions of the judges are final and binding.
